## üß† What Are AI Tools?
AI tools are software applications that generate responses based on user input (called a **prompt**).

AI tools can help with:
- Explaining concepts
- Answering questions
- Improving writing
- Summarizing information
- Learning step by step

AI tools do not think like humans.  
The **quality of the output depends on the quality of the prompt**.

### ‚úÖ Good use:

- Improving clarity

### ‚ùå Bad use:
